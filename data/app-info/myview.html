myView is a virtual desktop application (VDI) that gives you access to a GSA desktop from virtually any device with an Internet connection, including your smartphones and tablets.

Configuring the App

  1. Enter myview.gsa.gov in the Server Address field and click Add Server
  2. Accept the Terms of Service
  3. Enter your ENT username and password
  4. Enter your passcode from the SecureAuth OTP app
  5. Launch the virtual desktop by clicking the computer icon
