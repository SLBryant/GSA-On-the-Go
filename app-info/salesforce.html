The Salesforce1 App allows you to edit and create new Salesforce records right from your Mobile device. Stay connected to the people and records that matter most.

Configuring the App

  1. Open the Salesforce1 app and click on the gear icon in the top right corner
  2. Select the + at the top right corner to add a new connection
  3. Enter gsa.my.salesforce.com as the Host and GSA as the label and tap Done.
  4. Select GSA in the choose connection window.
