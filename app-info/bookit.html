Install the BookIT! app on an approved mobile device, and reserve conference rooms and workstations anywhere and anytime you are connected to the GSA-Wireless network.

Configure the App

  1. Open the OnBoard app and enter the information below
    Host Name: bookit.gsa.gov
    Use SSL: Enable
    Port: 443 (this is not editable)
  2. Enter your ENT Username and Password and log in
